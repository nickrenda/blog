I am a first year graduate student at Iowa State University in the Statistics Ph.D. program. I recently graduated from St. Lawrence University with a B.S. in Mathematics. While at St. Lawrence, I played for the varsity soccer team for my first three years and went to NCAA tournament every year. As part of my graduate assistantship this semester I am lecturing Stat 101: Principles of Statistics. I am also a part time tutor for mathematics, statistics, and computer science, and a part time web developer.

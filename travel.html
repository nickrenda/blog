In between summer and fall semesters at Iowa State, my parents and I took went on a 10-day trip to Europe. We spent a few days exploring Copenhagen before flying on to Dublin, where we rented a car and drove across the Irish countryside. Highlight of the trip: the Cliffs! Literally. Germany Summer 2015

I was invited to Berlin, Germany during the summer of 2015 when I was part of a team that placed 2nd in the Data Mining Cup. The Data Mining Cup, hosted by Prudsys AG, is an international data mining competition for universities. In 2015, a total of 188 teams from 48 countries participated in the challenge which involved predicting coupon usage rates and basket value for real costumers of an online shop.

Cross-Country Road Trip Summer 2014

Country music and the open road.

After graduating in May of 2014 from college, I drove across the country with my girlfriend at the time to see as many national parks as we could. We started off in Acadia National Park and made stops at Badlands, the Black Hills, Devil's Tower, Yellowstone, Glacier and finally Olympic National Park on the rocky shores of Washington. On the way back east we also went to Arches National Park in Utah and Rocky Mountain National Park in Colorado. Notable memories from the trip include seldom taking a shower since we camped out almost every night, barely making it over the Rockies in the Corolla, and biking Going-to-the-Sun Road in Glacier National Park.

China Spring 2013

During the Spring semester of my junior year as an undergrad I studied abroad in Shanghai, China. Throughout the trip I lived with a Chinese host family and travelled to Beijing (北京), Hong Kong (香港), Guangzhou (广州), Macau, and also Thailand. Notable memories include mastering charades to communicate with my host family at dinner, getting a decent meal for about $1.60, and learning the best way to use a squatting toilet.

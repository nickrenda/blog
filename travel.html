Europe Summer 2015

I went to Europe for the first time during the summer of 2015 when I was part of a team that placed 2nd out of 188 teams in the Data Mining Cup. The award ceremony was held in Berlin, Germany, and even though I was only there for a few days, I was able to see most of the city.

Cross-Country Road Trip Summer 2014

After graduating in May of 2014 from college, my girlfriend and I decided to drive across the country to see as many national parks as we could. We started off in Acadia National Park and made stops at Badlands, the Black Hills, Devil's Tower, Yellowstone, Glacier and finally Olympic National Park on the rocky shores of Washington. On the way back east we also went to Arches National Park and Rocky Mountain National Park in Colorado. Notable memories from the trip include seldom taking a shower since we camped out almost every night, barely making it over the Rockies in the Corolla, and biking Going-to-the-Sun Road in Glacier National Park.

China Spring 2013

The spring semester of my junior year of undergrad I studied abroad in Shanghai, China. During this time I lived with a Chinese host family and travelled to Beijing (北京), Hong Kong (香港), Guangzhou (广州), Macau, and also Thailand. Notable memories include daily struggles to communicate with my host family at dinner, learning the best way to use a squatting toilet, and meeting my girlfriend who liked me enough to take a month-long road trip with me.

Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am a first year graduate student at Iowa State University in the Statistics Ph.D. program. I recently graduated from St. Lawrence University with a B.S. in Mathematics. While at St. Lawrence, my honors thesis focused on applying statistics to a large amount of financial data to analyze the performance of momentum investing, a strategy that involves buying securities that have performed relatively well over the last period and selling those that have performed relatively poorly. You can view and download my finalized paper here, or navigate to the "research" tab and find it there.

Currently I am course lab instructor for Stat 101 and Stat 326.
